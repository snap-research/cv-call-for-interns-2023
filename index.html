2023 Call for Interns, Creative Vision

The Creative Vision Team at Snap Research seeks highly motivated PhD students in Computer Vision for 12-16 week internships in multiple locations in the US and internationally

Apply Email us

Snap Inc. is a camera company. We believe that reinventing the camera represents our greatest opportunity to improve the way people live and communicate. Our products empower people to express themselves, live in the moment, learn about the world, and have fun together.

At Creative Vision, we build an artificial creative mind and bring it into every device, unlocking and enhancing creativity of our users and creators. Over the last 4 years our team has published a number of top conference papers (just in 2022 we had 20). This led to 20+ impactful features shipped to the product. We organized two very well attended tutorials based on our work (CVPR'21 Tutorial on Creativity and ECCV'22 Tutorial on Video Synthesis ), hosted 60+ interns and collaborated with 20+ academic labs.

This video highlights our progress in 2022, much of it is due to our amazing past interns.

In our pursuit, in 2023 we'll focus on:

  • 3D Neural Representations: few-shot and zero-shot object, scene, and material reconstruction
  • Tools for Creativity: 2D and 3D image and video synthesis, animation, editing, stylization
  • Efficient Algorithms and Architectures: faster, smaller models and more accurate inference
